Prompt #1: Initial Product Analysis

Deep foundational research that analyzes all aspects of an Amazon product to support subsequent tasks including image creation, video production, copywriting optimization, and product improvement strategies.

**MARKETING INTELLIGENCE EXTRACTION** **CONTEXT:** Product URL: [YOUR_PRODUCT_URL] | Reviews: [FILE_ATTACHED/ALL_AVAILABLE] | Goal: Generate actionable insights for marketing decisions, creative assets, sales pages, and product improvements. **ROLE:** You are a senior marketing strategist specializing in customer intelligence for e-commerce optimization. **OUTPUT STRUCTURE:** **1. DATA FOUNDATION** (2-3 sentences) Analysis based on X reviews | Y% contain actionable insights | Z% demographic data **2. CUSTOMER SEGMENTS** (3 segments max, 20% minimum threshold) For each segment: - WHO: Specific characteristics + % of demographic base - WHY: Core motivations driving purchase - PAIN: Top 2 frustrations with meaningful quotes (up to 25 words) - LANGUAGE: Words/phrases they use + emotional triggers **3. MARKETING GOLDMINE** - WINNING MESSAGES: Top 5 phrases from satisfied customers - RED FLAGS: Top 3 complaint patterns to address - EMOTIONAL HOOKS: Specific feelings driving purchases - VISUAL CUES: What scenarios/settings customers describe - COMPETITIVE CONTEXT: How customers compare to alternatives **4. PRODUCT PERFORMANCE** - HERO FEATURES: Most praised aspects (with frequencies) - FAILURE POINTS: Critical issues to fix (with impact %) - MARKET POSITIONING: Customer-defined category position **5. IMMEDIATE ACTIONS** - CREATIVE BRIEF: 3 specific photo/video concepts with exact scenes - SALES PAGE: 2 headline options + 3 key benefits in customer language - PRODUCT FIXES: Top 2 urgent improvements with business impact - MESSAGING STRATEGY: Key themes to emphasize/avoid **QUALITY RULES:** ✓ All percentages from relevant subsets only - show calculation basis ✓ Real customer quotes (15-25 words for emotional impact) ✓ Minimum 20% threshold for segments ✓ Include competitive mentions and positioning insights ✓ Focus on immediately actionable insights ✓ No speculation - data-driven only **OUTPUT LENGTH:** 900-1300 words maximum Start analysis now.
