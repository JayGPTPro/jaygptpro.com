What She Does

10 Skills. One Employee.

Every skill is a one-word command that replaces a paragraph of instructions. Click any skill to see what it does.

☀️

Morning Briefing

Waiting for you every morning, automatically.

Scans your emails, calendar, and tasks every morning. Categorizes emails by urgency, flags conflicts, identifies your #1 priority for the day. Runs automatically at the time you set.
✉️

Draft Replies

Writes emails in your voice. You just press send.

Drafts a reply to any email in your personal writing style. Checks your relationship history with the sender, open commitments, and tone preferences. Never sends without your approval.
📋

Meeting Prep

Full brief before every meeting. Automatic.

Researches who you're meeting by pulling email history, calendar history, and shared documents. Builds a brief with who they are, what you discussed last time, open items, and suggested agenda.
🤝

Accountability

Tracks your promises. Calls you out.

Detects promises from your emails and meetings. Tracks them in both directions: what you owe others, and what others owe you. Flags overdue commitments with how many days late. Honest assessment, no sugarcoating.
🔍

Forgotten Emails

Finds what fell through the cracks. Both ways.

Scans your inbox for emails you forgot to answer (3+ days) and emails where others haven't replied to you. Drafts follow-up messages for both. Flags anything involving money or deadlines.
⚠️

Risk Detection

Flags problems before they explode.

Connects the dots across emails, calendar, tasks, and promises. Deadline approaching + task not started = risk. Multiple overdue items for one client = relationship risk. Financial setup incomplete + launch date = revenue risk.
📅

Block Time

Blocks calendar time for tasks you keep avoiding.

When a task has been postponed 3+ times, she finds a free slot on your calendar and blocks it. No more excuses. She picks the best time based on your meeting load and energy patterns.
🌙

Evening Summary

End-of-day wrap. What's done, what's not, what's tomorrow.

Reviews what got done today and what didn't. Checks for new emails since morning. Preps you for tomorrow's meetings. Suggests priorities for the next day. Runs automatically every evening.
📊

Weekly Report

Every Sunday. Wins, misses, patterns, next week.

Full weekly analysis: tasks completed vs created, promises kept vs broken, meeting load, patterns, week-over-week comparison, and top 3 priorities for next week. Data-backed, not vague.
🧠

Gets Smarter Daily

Every interaction makes her sharper.

Reviews what worked and what didn't. Updates her memory with new patterns, preferences, and corrections. In a month, she knows you better than you know yourself.
