Cannot get in?

90% of login problems are one of these three

1. You paid with a different email

Your key is your Google account. If you paid at checkout with one email but signed in with another Google account, the door will not recognize you. Sign out, then sign in with the Google account that matches your payment email. If your payment email is not a Google account at all, email us and we will link it (takes minutes).

2. You literally just paid

Access activates within a minute of payment. Wait 60 seconds, refresh, try again.

3. Your subscription lapsed

If a payment failed, we retry automatically for two weeks and email you. Update your card from the email link, or rejoin from the pricing page.

Still stuck? Email support@jaygptpro.com with the email you paid with. A human answers.
