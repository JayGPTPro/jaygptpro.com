Privacy Policy

AI Vault for eCom Sellers · Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

  • Account: your name, email, and profile photo from Google sign-in.
  • Billing: handled by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. We store your subscription status and billing history references.
  • Usage: which episodes you watch and your playback position (so Continue Watching works), lesson completion, page views inside the portal, and basic sign-in events (approximate location, device type) for account security.

2. What we use it for

  • Running your membership: access, progress, receipts, support.
  • Transactional email (welcome, payment issues) and, if you opt in, member updates. Every non-essential email has an unsubscribe link that we honor immediately.
  • Improving the Vault: aggregate viewing stats tell us which content works.

We do not sell your data. We do not run third-party advertising trackers inside the portal.

3. Who processes it (subprocessors)

  • Supabase (database, authentication)
  • Stripe (payments)
  • Vimeo (video hosting)
  • Resend (email delivery)
  • GitHub Pages (site hosting)
  • Google (sign-in)

4. Your rights (including GDPR/UK GDPR)

You can request a copy of your data, correction, or full deletion at any time: email support@jaygptpro.com from your account email. Deletion removes your profile and usage data within 30 days; billing records are retained as long as tax law requires. EU/UK members: our lawful basis is contract performance (running your membership) and legitimate interest (security, product improvement).

5. Retention and security

Account and usage data live for the life of your membership plus 6 months (so returning members keep their history), then are deleted on request or per schedule. Sign-in security logs are kept 90 days. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by our subprocessors.
