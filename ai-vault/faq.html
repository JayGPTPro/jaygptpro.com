Getting around

How do episodes work?

One new live deep dive every month on Zoom. The replay lands in Episodes the same day, with the prompts, files, and links attached under "In the case". You have instant access to the entire back catalog from day one.

Where do I start?

Start Here. Eight short lessons, and lesson two gives you your first win in about ten minutes: one tested prompt running on your own listing.

Where are the prompts and tools?

Two compartments on the Vault Floor: the Prompt Vault (save, organize, reuse) and the AI Toolbox (the tools that work right now). Plus every episode carries its own resources.

What are the WhatsApp groups?

Two groups: the Vault Community (the daily conversation, members and experts) and Updates Only (announcements, zero chatter). Join one or both from the WhatsApp page.

How do live sessions and Masterminds work?

The Upcoming page shows everything with countdowns, your local time, add-to-calendar buttons, and the Zoom links. Episodes are workshops; Masterminds are member hot seats, once a month.

Your membership

Account page → Manage billing → Cancel. One click, self serve, no phone calls. Access stays until the end of what you paid for.

What is the guarantee?

30 days, money back, no forms and no questions. Applies to your first payment, once per person. Full details on the refund policy page.

Will my price ever go up?

No. The price you join at is yours for as long as your subscription stays active, even when the list price rises.

I paid but cannot get in

Ninety percent of the time you signed in with a different Google account than the email you paid with. The help page walks you through it, and support@jaygptpro.com answers fast.
