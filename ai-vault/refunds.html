Refund and Cancellation Policy

AI Vault for eCom Sellers · Last updated: July 2026

The 30-day guarantee

Join the Vault. If within 30 days of your first payment you decide it is not for you, we refund that payment in full. No forms, no questions, no hard feelings. Email support@jaygptpro.com from the email you joined with, or ask in your account page, and the refund is issued to your original payment method within 5 business days.

  • Applies to your FIRST payment (monthly or annual), once per person, lifetime.
  • After a guarantee refund, access ends immediately.
  • Rejoining later is always welcome, at the then-current price.

Cancellation

  • Cancel anytime, yourself, in one click: account page → Manage billing → Cancel.
  • No cancellation fees, no retention phone calls, no chatbots.
  • Your access continues until the end of the period you already paid for. Renewals after the 30-day window are not refunded, they are simply stopped by cancelling.
  • Annual members: you keep access for the full paid year after cancelling.

Failed payments

If a renewal payment fails we retry automatically for up to two weeks and email you. Your access stays on during retries. If payment cannot be collected, the subscription ends without any debt on your side.

Double charges and mistakes

Any billing mistake on our side (double charge, wrong amount) is refunded the same day it is reported. Report to support@jaygptpro.com.
