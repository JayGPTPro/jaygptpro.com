What's inside

One short paragraph per skill, so you know what each one does before you use it.

/create-sop

Turn any recurring task into a ready-to-use SOP

Tell Donna about a task you do over and over. She breaks it into clear numbered steps, lists the tools, time estimate, and common pitfalls. The result is a doc you can drop into Drive and hand to anyone.

Use it the next time you catch yourself doing the same thing for the third week in a row.
/quality-check

Quality control before you publish anything

Before sending a post, email, proposal, or landing page, Donna checks it against your voice, common AI tells, and content quality. She gives one of three verdicts: approved, send back with specific fixes, or escalate.

Run it on every important piece of content you write. You will catch things you'd otherwise ship.
/remember + /recall

Long-term business memory

Tell Donna "remember that we decided X" and she stores it. Later, ask "what did we decide about pricing in February?" and she retrieves it with full context: who, why, what led to it.

Decisions, deals, numbers, key context. Stop relying on memory.
/subscriptions

Find subscriptions you pay for and don't use

Donna scans your inbox for recurring charges, builds a list with monthly totals, flags upcoming renewals, and surfaces unknown charges that look like new subscriptions sneaking in.

Almost everyone running this finds at least $30/month they didn't know they were spending.
/contracts

Track contracts and alert before expiry

Maintains a list of active contracts: who, what, dates, auto-renew, alert window. Flags anything expiring soon in your morning briefing so you decide to renew, renegotiate, or cancel before it's automatic.

Especially good if you have vendor contracts, employee agreements, or office leases.
/patterns

Behavioral patterns Donna spots in your data

Donna analyzes 30 days of your calendar, tasks, and emails. She surfaces 2-3 actionable insights you wouldn't notice yourself. "You cancel 40% of meetings after 8pm." "Tasks tagged 'admin' take 3x longer than 'content'."

Run weekly. Patterns become habits become your operating system.
/invoices

Auto-detect invoices and receipts in your inbox

Scans Gmail for invoices, receipts, and payment confirmations. Lists them with vendor, amount, date, ready for bookkeeping. Picks up Stripe, PayPal, and any vendor email with attached PDFs.

Run before month-end close. Saves an hour of digging.
/promises

Track promises you made to other people

When you tell someone "I'll send you that by Friday", Donna logs it. She flags overdue ones in your morning briefing and offers to draft a message or extend the deadline.

Different from your own task list. This is what others are waiting for from you.
/tasks-from-emails

Turn an email into an actionable task

Find an email, Donna reads it, extracts the action, sets a deadline, picks the best project, and proposes a task in your task manager (Asana, Todoist, Notion, whatever you use). You approve.

No more "I'll get to it later" emails sitting forever in the inbox.
/stuck

Alert on tasks that haven't moved

Pulls your incomplete tasks, flags any that have been sitting 10+ days without progress. Three options for each: do it today, delegate it, or kill it. No middle ground.

Your "I'll get to it" list is killing you. This is the cleanup.
