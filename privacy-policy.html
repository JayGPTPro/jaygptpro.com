Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Jay GPT PRO LLC ("we", "us") handles your information when you use our services, including the Donna Challenge portal and related applications.

What We Collect

When you sign in using Google OAuth, we receive:

  • Your Google email address
  • Your name (as set in your Google account)

We do not access your Google Drive, contacts, calendar, or any other Google services beyond basic profile information needed for login.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and grant access to the Donna Challenge portal
  • To associate your progress and content within the challenge
  • To send you relevant updates about the challenge (only if you opted in)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used solely for providing access to our services.

Data Storage

Your authentication data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing info@jaygptpro.com.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only for authentication session management. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
