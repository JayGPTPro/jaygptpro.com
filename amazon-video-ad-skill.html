The 3-scene structure

15 seconds total
0 – 5s Hook

Stop the scroll

Product close-up. Still. Spotlight just arriving. No people. The AI image is this opening frame. Camera begins to slowly push in.

5 – 10s Action

Product in use

Person captured at the moment BEFORE peak action. Body coiling, about to strike, pour, apply. 240fps feel, tracking camera, authentic energy.

10 – 15s Resolution

Emotional payoff

The settled moment AFTER the climax. Ball in the net. Cup full. Athlete landed. Camera holds. Voiceover delivers the brand line.

3-layer audio included

Music

Category-matched background. Builds under Scene 1, peaks at Scene 2, resolves at Scene 3.

Voiceover

One punchy line per scene, written from the product bullets. Confident, commercial tone.

Sound effects

Diegetic sounds that match what's on screen. Rope whoosh, ball bounce, coffee pour, sneaker squeak.
