Step 1

Strategic Blueprint

Paste this into ChatGPT with your product URL and photos attached.

Prompt 1
Strategic Blueprint
Analyzes your listing · builds the visual plan
Paste in ChatGPT 
You are an expert Amazon listing image strategist and commercial creative director.

Analyze the product I give you and create a complete visual plan for 7 Amazon listing images. Your goal: images that are beautiful, consistent, professional, and built to convert.

WHAT I WILL GIVE YOU:
- Amazon product URL (or title + bullet points)
- Product images [attach them]

YOUR OUTPUT — FOLLOW THIS STRUCTURE EXACTLY:

SECTION 1: PRODUCT READ
- What is this product and who buys it (2 sentences)
- Top 5 visual features worth showing in images
- The one sentence that captures what makes this product feel good to own

SECTION 2: VISUAL SYSTEM
- Primary colors (hex codes if possible, otherwise descriptive)
- Secondary / accent colors
- Mood and style: write 3 words (e.g. "energetic, party, fun" or "clean, premium, minimal")
- Lighting direction for all images
- Consistent element to repeat across every image

SECTION 3: 7-IMAGE PLAN
For each image:
- Number and type (Main Shot / Lifestyle / Infographic / etc.)
- Visual concept in 2 sentences
- Who is in the scene if anyone (age, energy, setting)
- Any text or callout to include

Images to plan:
1. Main product shot — white background, product fills 75%, no text, no props
2. Hero lifestyle — product in use, real person, authentic moment, emotionally compelling
3. Feature infographic — 3–5 callouts showing key benefits, clean and graphic
4. Lifestyle scene 2 — different angle, different person or setting, same mood
5. Benefits list — numbered or icon-based benefits, strong headline
6. Gift or celebration scene — product as a gift or party prop, warm and exciting
7. Detail close-up — extreme quality shot showing the best physical detail

END WITH: "The consistent visual language across all 7 images is: ___."

Do not ask questions. Use whatever I give you and deliver the full plan now.

[PASTE YOUR AMAZON URL HERE — or paste title + bullet points]
[ATTACH YOUR PRODUCT IMAGES]
Open ChatGPT ↗
💡 Attach 2–3 existing product photos. ChatGPT sees the product and images become much more accurate.
Step 2

Image Generation Prompts

Paste this in the same chat after getting the strategy.

Prompt 2
Image Generation Prompts
Same chat · 7 prompts ready to generate
Same chat 
Based on the visual strategy above, now write 7 professional image generation prompts.

Each prompt will be used directly in ChatGPT to generate the image — make them detailed, visual, and specific.

RULES FOR EVERY PROMPT:
- 100–150 words each
- Start with: "Amazon listing image, professional commercial photography, [image type]."
- Describe exactly: what's in the frame, how the product is positioned, lighting, mood, colors
- Reference the consistent visual system you defined above
- End every prompt with: "Visual consistency: [color palette], [mood/style], [lighting]."
- For images with people: describe real, natural-looking humans — not stock photo models
- Never crop the product or cut it off at the edges
- Image 1: white background, product only, no text, no props, product fills 75% of frame

FORMAT:
IMAGE 1: [Type]
[Prompt]

IMAGE 2: [Type]
[Prompt]

...continue for all 7...

After all 7, write exactly:
"Ready. Type 'Generate image 1' to start, or 'Generate all 7' to begin the full set."
💡 Once you have the 7 prompts, type "Generate image 1". ChatGPT will use your product photos as reference. Go one by one — takes 3–4 minutes total.
Pro Tips

Get Better Results

✓Make 50, keep 7. The more options you generate, the better your final set. Quantity leads to quality.
✓Two versions of your main image. One conservative (Amazon-safe), one more creative. A/B test which converts better.
✓Stay in one chat. The longer the conversation, the better ChatGPT understands your product. Never start fresh mid-session.
✓Lock the style early. The visual system from Prompt 1 stays consistent through all 7 images automatically.
